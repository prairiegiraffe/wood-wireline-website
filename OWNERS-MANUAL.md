## ðŸ“§ Why Am I Not Getting Emails?

**The most common reason you're not receiving form submission emails is because email notifications are turned off for your account.**

### How to Turn On Email Notifications

1. Log into the admin dashboard (see above)
2. Click **Settings** in the top navigation bar
3. Scroll down to **Email Notifications** section
4. Under "Notify me about", select one of the following options:
   - **None** - You won't receive any emails (this is probably why you're not getting them!)
   - **Contact forms only** - Only get emails when someone submits a contact form
   - **Job applications only** - Only get emails when someone submits a job application
   - **All form submissions** - Get emails for both contact forms and job applications
5. Click **Save Notification Preferences**

**Note:** Emails are sent to the email address associated with your account. Check your spam/junk folder if you still don't see them.

## ðŸ‘¥ How to Add a New User

You can add team members who need access to view or manage form submissions.

### Steps to Add a User:

1. Log into the admin dashboard
2. Click **Users** in the top navigation bar
3. Click the **Add User** button (blue button in top right)
4. Fill out the user information:
   - **Name**: Full name of the user
   - **Email**: Their email address (this is their login username)
   - **Password**: Create a password for them (minimum 8 characters)
   - **Role**: Choose their access level:
     - **Admin** - Can view, manage submissions, and add/edit users
     - **Viewer** - Can only view submissions (read-only access)
   - **Email Notifications**: Choose what form submissions they should be notified about
5. Click **Save User**

The new user can now log in with their email and the password you created.

## âœï¸ How to Edit an Existing User

1. Log into the admin dashboard
2. Click **Users** in the top navigation
3. Find the user you want to edit
4. Click **Edit** next to their name
5. Make your changes:
   - Update their name
   - Change their access level (Admin or Viewer)
   - **Change their email notification settings** (this is what you need if they're not getting emails!)
   - Reset their password (leave blank to keep their current password)
   - Activate or deactivate their account
6. Click **Save User**
